Introduction

OpenLink Virtuoso is a multi-purpose platform for data access, integration, virtualization, and management. It's multi-model and multi-protocol architecture enables the delivery of unique functionality in areas such as:

  1. Database Management (Tables and Graphs)
  2. Data Virtualization across Data Silos
  3. Data Mesh Construction & Deployment
  4. Knowledge Graph Construction & Deployment
  5. Modern Identity & Access Management
